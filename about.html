About me

My career path in a few lines

My name is Catarina, and I'm Portuguese. I studied social communication and multimedia communication, specializing in digital audiovisual. I have worked in television and film studio production, with various teams and people of different ages, from whom I learned a lot.

Image editing (photography and video) has always fascinated me, so I took a motion design course that immersed me in a creative world that I love.

I worked for a company that created websites, and at that time, I had no idea what awaited me. My initiation into coding started as an experiment, and I had no idea I would love it! It's an area that constantly challenges me, and thanks to the workshops by SheCodes, I discovered that it's a thrilling journey.
